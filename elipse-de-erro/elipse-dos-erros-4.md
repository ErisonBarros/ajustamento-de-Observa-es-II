# Elipse dos Erros



$$
M^2 = 4\sigma_{xy}^2 + (\sigma_x^2 - \sigma_y^2)^2
$$

$$
\sigma^2 = 0.5(\sigma_x^2 + \sigma_y^2 \pm M)
$$

$$
\tan(2t) = \frac{2\sigma_{xy}}{\sigma_x^2 - \sigma_y^2}
$$

$$
\sin(2t) = \frac{2\sigma_{xy}}{M}
$$

$$
\cos(2t) = \frac{\sigma_x^2 - \sigma_y^2}{M}
$$
